# SuperSuperMarket Sales Dashboard

A multi-page interactive sales dashboard built for my Information Technology Management course.
The dashboard visualizes regional and product sales data for a fictional supermarket chain
across four U.S. cities (Boston, Los Angeles, New York, San Diego) over 2024–2025.

## 🔗 Live Site
[View the dashboard here](https://your-username.github.io/your-repo-name)

## 📄 Pages

- **Regional Performance** – KPI cards, revenue by region (East vs West), monthly revenue
  trends, and units sold by product per region. Includes a filter form for analyst use.
- **Annual Sales Trends** – Year-over-year sales breakdown by city for 2024 and 2025,
  with data tables and bar charts side by side.
- **Product Performance** – Top products by revenue and quantity sold, revenue by category,
  and key business insights and suggestions.

## 🛠️ Built With

- HTML5
- CSS3 (custom styles + Bootstrap 5)
- Charts created in Google Sheets and embedded as images

## 🎨 Design Choices

The dashboard uses a consistent light blue (`#D2F7FF`) card style across all pages,
a green header banner (`#D1FFCA`), and a navy blue (`#2F426D`) color palette for text
and buttons. Visual hierarchy, balance, contrast, and gestalt principles (proximity and
similarity) were applied throughout to guide the user's eye from high-level KPIs down
to detailed charts and insights.
